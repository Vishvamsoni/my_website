Common Questions

What is the lifespan of solar panels? +
Most premium panels we install come with a 25-year performance warranty and can last up to 30-35 years with minimal maintenance.
Does solar work during monsoon? +
Yes! While production is lower on cloudy days, solar panels still generate electricity from ambient light. Rain also helps clean the panels for better efficiency.
What is Net Metering? +
Net Metering allows you to send excess energy back to the MGVCL/GUVNL grid and receive credits on your bill for the units you contribute.
